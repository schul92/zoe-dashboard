Strategic Decisions & Recommendations

Decision 1: Size Strategy

‚úÖ Recommendation: Small Dog Focus (Phase 1: 6 months)

Target 5-25 lbs dogs with 3 sizes (XS/S/M). Small dogs have 70-80% apparel adoption rate vs. 30-40% for larger breeds.

Key breeds: Chihuahua, Pomeranian, Yorkie, Maltese, Shih Tzu, Mini Poodle, French Bulldog

‚úÖ Recommendation: Mid-Range Balance ($35-55 price point)

Risk Mitigation Strategies

Returns Management

  • Size guides: Breed-specific measurement charts (reduce "doesn't fit" returns)
  • Photo reviews: Show products on real dogs of different breeds
  • Exchange-first policy: Offer size exchanges before refunds
  • Store credit bonus: 110% credit vs. 100% cash refund
  • 30-day quality guarantee: Free replacement for defects

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Reduction

  • Organic social: TikTok/IG Reels (3-5 posts/week) - cute dog videos
  • Email marketing: Capture 30% of visitors, nurture with Klaviyo
  • Referral program: 10% off for referrer + referee
  • Micro-influencers: Send free products to pet influencers (500-5K followers)
  • SEO content: Blog posts ("Best Sweaters for Chihuahuas," "Raincoat Sizing Guide")
  • Target: Reduce ad spend from 22% to 15% by month 6
